Project Overview

This project focuses on creating a user-centered caregiving web platform developed for the Candlelight Foundation for Children with Special Needs (CLSN). The goal was to design and build a responsive digital system that bridges the connection between clients, caregivers, and the foundation—ensuring better communication, accountability, and accessibility of specialized care services.

Caregiving Management Web Application

The CLSN Caregiving Platform is a comprehensive web application designed to streamline care coordination for individuals with special needs. The platform allows clients to register for caregiving services, caregivers to manage their assigned clients, and administrators to oversee the entire care delivery process in real time.

The Caregiving Management System was conceptualized as a digital transformation initiative to support the foundation’s mission of providing structured, transparent, and compassionate care to individuals with special needs. The system delivers a seamless interface for all stakeholders—enhancing service delivery, accountability, and data management through intuitive design and robust backend architecture.

  • Multi-user access (Clients, Caregivers, Admins)
  • Real-time scheduling and task tracking
  • Secure database for client and caregiver profiles
  • Responsive, accessibility-focused UI/UX design
