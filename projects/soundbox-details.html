SoundBox — Music Streaming & Audio Discovery App

SoundBox empowers users to stream and enjoy audio content seamlessly through an elegant and responsive Flutter interface. With music sourced from various regions, users can connect with diverse cultural sounds while earning rewards through in-app listening engagement.

The app integrates a secure PHP/MySQL backend that manages user data, streaming content, and point systems for reward distribution. From song upload to playlist management, SoundBox delivers a community-driven audio experience that combines entertainment, discovery, and earning potential.

  • Stream and discover global music and podcasts
  • Earn rewards (NCR credits) while listening
  • Personalized playlists and user profiles
  • Admin tools for managing artists and uploads
  • Built using Flutter, PHP, and MySQL
